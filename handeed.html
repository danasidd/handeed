The Jordanian website is dedicated to showcasing local businesses that sell traditional and cultural products commonly found in downtown Amman. From handcrafted jewelry and embroidered clothing to unique ceramics and artisanal goods, the platform highlights the rich heritage of Jordan through the crafts and artistry of its people. Each business is carefully selected to offer authentic products that reflect the country’s vibrant culture. Users can explore a variety of items, learn about the artisans, and even find information about the shops' locations in the bustling markets of downtown Amman. The website serves as a bridge between local craftsmanship and customers who appreciate the cultural treasures of Jordan
